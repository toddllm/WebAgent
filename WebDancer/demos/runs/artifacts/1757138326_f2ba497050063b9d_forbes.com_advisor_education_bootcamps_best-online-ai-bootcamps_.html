          Completing an artificial intelligence bootcamp online can accelerate your promotion or entry into the job you want, but there are some things you need to consider before you make your choice.

          To help you find the best training program, we evaluated all online AI bootcamps that meet our ranking criteria. Learn about the best AI bootcamps below, as well as salary and outcomes data from a 2023 Forbes Advisor bootcamp survey and bootcamp statistics from across the country.

          Why You Can Trust Forbes Advisor Education

          Forbes Advisor’s education editors are committed to producing unbiased rankings and informative articles covering online colleges, tech bootcamps and career paths. Our ranking methodologies use data from the National Center for Education Statistics, education providers, and reputable educational and professional organizations. An advisory board of educators and other subject matter experts reviews and verifies our content to bring you trustworthy, up-to-date information. Advertisers do not influence our rankings or editorial content.

          • Over 3,868 accredited, nonprofit colleges and universities analyzed nationwide
          • 52 reputable tech bootcamp providers evaluated for our rankings
          • All content is fact-checked and updated on an annual basis
          • Rankings undergo five rounds of fact-checking
          • Only 7.12% of all colleges, universities and bootcamp providers we consider are awarded

          Our Methodology

          We ranked five bootcamps in the U.S. using 14 data points in the categories of career support, credibility, affordability and student experience. We pulled data for these categories from individual bootcamp providers. Data is accurate as of June 2025.

          We scored bootcamps on a points system based on the following metrics:

          Career Support:

          • Access to career coaching or mentorship
          • Availability of job guarantee
          • Level of career services support

          Credibility:

          • Council on Integrity in Results Reporting (CIRR) membership status
          • Student outcomes data transparency
          • Pricing transparency
          • Length of time provider has been operating

          Affordability:

          • Upfront cost
          • Availability of installment payment plan
          • Availability of delayed payment plan
          • Scholarships available
          • Accepts GI Bill®

          Student Experience:

          • Average time to completion (in hours)
          • Scheduling flexibility

          We listed all five bootcamps in the U.S. that met our ranking criteria.

          For the purpose of our rankings, we do not evaluate self-paced bootcamps. Find our full list of methodologies here.

          What To Expect From an AI Bootcamp

          AI bootcamps are intensive and focused training programs designed to teach students how to work with AI and machine learning models and technologies. Depending on the program and study schedule, your training in an AI bootcamp for beginners may take less than a month or up to one year.

          Many AI bootcamps run entirely online, and classes often run synchronously to provide the most engaging and interactive experience possible. Along with class lectures, your bootcamp may include hands-on projects as well as group discussions and assignments.

          Each of our ranked bootcamps also provides mentorship and extensive career services support to personalize your experience and improve your career opportunities.

          Admission Requirements

          Admission requirements for AI bootcamps vary by program. Some tackle advanced material and require specific skills or professional experience from candidates. The bootcamps on our list are all beginner-friendly and demand little to no previous experience or prerequisites.

          What You’ll Learn

          Most AI bootcamps teach the fundamentals of AI and how to use the most popular machine learning technologies for various applications. Along the way, students can develop programming, data science and analytical skills.

          Your exact courses may vary in title and substance, but courses in AI bootcamps commonly address subjects such as:

          • Natural language processing
          • Prompt engineering
          • Python for data science
          • Large language models
          • Data analysis
          • Deep learning

          What Can You Do With an AI Bootcamp?

          The widespread usage of AI across many industries has made skilled AI practitioners a hot commodity. AI bootcamp graduates can apply their skills to many computer, information technology and business intelligence occupations, with titles such as:

          • Information research scientists
          • Data scientists
          • Software developers and engineers
          • Computer programmers
          • Operations research analysts
          • Market research analysts

          While every employer has its own experience and credential requirements, you may need a college degree in addition to completing an AI bootcamp to obtain these careers.

          Should You Enroll in an AI Bootcamp?

          Pro Tip
          AI bootcamps usually feature set class times, so make sure you can fit your studies into your schedule.

          The intensified focus and short completion timeline of AI bootcamps help them keep up with the speed of technological advances. These condensed programs hone in on the most relevant technologies and career-specific training that can help you fast-track your way to a promotion or a career change, but they won’t appeal to everyone.

          AI bootcamps can be rigorous and time consuming. Even part-time programs can require 20 hours of study a week or more. Since classes typically run synchronously, you need enough schedule flexibility that you can set aside specific hours each week to devote to the bootcamp.

          AI Bootcamp Credibility

          Bootcamps lack the industry oversight and regulation that college and university degree programs have with accreditors, which can make it difficult to evaluate them. The Council on Integrity in Results Reporting (CIRR) helps by monitoring bootcamp providers and reporting on student outcomes.

          CIRR tracks bootcamp graduation rates, along with graduate employment rates and salaries. While it doesn’t have data for every bootcamp provider, CIRR reports on some major players in the industry.

          How To Find the Right AI Bootcamp for You

          Pro Tip
          Check to see whether your employer offers funding for continuing education programs as a form of professional development.

          To find the right AI bootcamp for you, consider how well the program fits into your life and prepares you for your future. As you conduct your search, ask yourself the following questions.

          What Are My Career Goals?

          If you have a career destination in mind, find an AI bootcamp that points you in that direction. Look for a program that helps you meet the skill and tool requirements for your target role. You should even consider the bootcamp length to ensure you can finish in an appropriate amount of time.

          The level of career services provided by your bootcamp is another useful consideration. For example, 4Geeks Academy offers unlimited career support for as long as you need, while some others restrict the number and dates of your mentoring sessions, providing career support only while you’re in the bootcamp.

          What’s My Availability?

          With AI bootcamps running synchronous online classes, you need to align your study schedule with your work schedule and other commitments. The programs we ranked run on full-time days throughout the week or part time during weekday evenings or weekend days.

          Along with online classes, you need to factor in time for independent study and completing your projects. You also have to accommodate for class discussions and mentorship sessions.

          What’s My Budget?

          AI bootcamps may be shorter than a typical college program, but they can carry a heavy price tag. The bootcamps on this list cost between $3,000 to $12,900 if learners pay tuition up front.

          Most providers offer installment or financing plans that can help you spread your expenses over a longer period of time, but it may increase the total bootcamp cost. In some cases, you can qualify for tuition discounts or scholarships that reduce your out-of-pocket expenses. Check to see whether your bootcamp of interest offers scholarships or discounts for which you may qualify.

          Paying for an AI Bootcamp

          Unlike college and university programs, bootcamps do not typically qualify for federal financial aid. However, there are financial support programs available that can make it easier to afford your bootcamp.

          How Much Is an AI Bootcamp?

          According to Forbes Advisor data collected in November 2023, the median cost of bootcamps is $8,300. Depending on your payment method, the tuition price for the bootcamp you choose may differ from the price you actually pay.

          Upfront Payment

          Most bootcamp providers offer their lowest prices to students who pay up front in one lump sum. Upfront payments also allow learners to avoid interest fees and tuition increases.

          Pay in Installments

          Many bootcamps, including the AI bootcamps on this list, allow students to pay their tuition in installments—some with interest and some without, depending on the payment plan. Breaking up the total cost into monthly payments can soften the financial blow initially, but in many cases it costs more than an upfront payment.

          Income Share Agreement

          While none of the AI bootcamps on our list offer income share agreements (ISAs), it’s important for you to understand how these payment plans typically work. In lieu of upfront payments, some bootcamp providers give students the option to pay via a percentage of their salary once they secure a job.

          The problem with this method, according to the Student Borrower Protection Center, is that many ISAs include contractual traps and fees that can hurt student credit and cost them much more than they originally believed.

          Job Guarantee

          Some bootcamps, including the 4Geeks Academy Applied AI program on this list, promise tuition refunds to students who cannot get a job after graduation. While this demonstrates provider confidence in the bootcamp and commitment to graduates, bootcamp job guarantees often come with stipulations.

          In some cases, job guarantees can be nullified if you turn down a position you deem unsuitable for your qualifications. You may also need to satisfy a list of specific conditions to remain eligible for the refund.

          Loans, Scholarships and Other Aid

          While federal aid may not be an option for most bootcamp students, providers often have low-interest personal loans available through a third-party partner. Some bootcamps offer scholarships to deserving students as well.

          If it applies to you, your bootcamp may also qualify for an employer tuition assistance program or VA benefits coverage.

          Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) About AI Bootcamps

          What jobs can you land after an AI/ML bootcamp?

          The bootcamps we ranked prepare students for different career paths, but some possible jobs for graduates include software engineer, data scientist and market research analyst.

          What is the job placement rate for AI coding bootcamps?

          According to a 2023 Forbes Advisor bootcamp survey, more than three quarters of bootcamp graduates found a job or advanced their career after completing their program. Only two bootcamp providers on our list—4Geeks Academy and Fullstack Academy—publicly provide student outcome data, and they each report graduate placement rates of 72% and higher. This data may not be specific to AI bootcamps only, however, but reflective of various coding bootcamps.

          How much can you make with an AI bootcamp?

          Bootcamp graduate salaries depend on many factors, including student experience, location and credentials, but Fullstack Academy, for example, reports a graduate median salary of $90,000. Many bootcamp students look to advance their careers, and Forbes Advisor’s survey found that bootcamp graduates increased their salary by an average of $27,920.

          Why are AI coding bootcamps popular in 2025?

          According to a 2025 Stanford University Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence report, AI integration and investment are commonplace across most industries and are increasingly part of daily life. AI bootcamps allow professionals to update and future-proof their skills quickly to help meet the demand these technologies require.
