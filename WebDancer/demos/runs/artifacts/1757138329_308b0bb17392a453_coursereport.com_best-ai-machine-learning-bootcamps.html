    What You Learn in an AI/Machine Learning Bootcamp

    The schools on this list offer AI and Machine Learning bootcamps, which are immersive, career-focused programs designed to train you to become an AI Engineer or Machine Learning Engineer. You’ll dive deep into machine learning algorithms, neural networks, and AI system design – not just how to prompt ChatGPT. Expect the curriculum to cover topics like large language models (LLMs), deep learning, AI governance, APIs, agent workflows, and advanced prompt engineering. They may cover AI languages and frameworks, such as TensorFlow, Python, and SQL.

    Every program on this list teaches artificial intelligence and machine learning in enough depth to prepare you for an entry-level career in the field. While some of these schools may market their programs as “Data Science” or “Software Engineering” bootcamps, we vetted their curricula to ensure they cover essential AI/ML skills.

    How to Choose The Best AI/Machine Learning Bootcamp for You

    Remember, there is no ultimate "best AI boot camp" – the best AI or machine learning course for you depends on your own learning style, career goals, availability, etc. No matter how many accolades a school has, make sure you do your research: read reviews, talk to alumni, take an intro course, and ask about job outcomes data. 

    This list is not a ranking, but rather AI/ML programs are listed alphabetically. Marketing partnerships that Course Report may have with schools on this list bear no weight in the ranking process. Schools cannot pay for inclusion on this list.

      Ranking Methodology

      As the leading authority on tech bootcamps, the team at Course Report has been researching, tracking, and sharing updates on this industry since 2013. As the artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) career paths continue to rise in popularity, we’re using our knowledge and expertise to curate the top AI/ML bootcamps around the world. Put simply, these are the AI/ML schools we would recommend to our own family and friends.

      To earn a spot on this list, schools must offer immersive learning – think dedicated career services, 20+ hours of weekly commitment, live instruction or dedicated mentors, and student support. 

      We look for the following qualities when selecting schools for this Best Bootcamps list:

      • Alumni + Student Reviews: Students and alumni have left more than 60,000 reviews on Course Report. We consider both quality & quantity of bootcamp reviews, and particular weight is given to Verified Reviews and reviews of a school’s AI/Machine Learning courses.

      • Time Commitment: AI/ML bootcamps included in this list have programs where students dedicate at least 20 hours of study a week and are at least 8 weeks long.

      • Varied Financing Options: Bootcamps that offer a variety of payment options like deferred tuition or school financing to make their program more accessible to students.

      • Vetted Lending Partnerships: Whether a school has been vetted and approved to work with reputable financing companies like Ascent Funding and Climb Credit.

      • In addition to the above factors, we considered a school's operation time, number of campuses, selectivity in the application process, caliber of career support, and commitment to diversity when creating this list.

      FAQs About Best AI/ML Bootcamps

      What is an Artifical Intelligence/Machine Learning Bootcamp

      An Artificial Intelligence (AI) / Machine Learning (ML) Bootcamp teaches students the technologies and skills they need to begin careers in AI and ML. An AI/ML Bootcamp will cover programming languages like Python, SQL, and TensorFlow. Students will learn about large language models (LLMs), deep learning, AI governance, APIs, agent workflows, and advanced prompt engineering.

      What jobs can you land after an AI/ML Bootcamp?

      AI and machine learning bootcamps prepare students for tech roles, such as AI Engineer or Machine Learning Engineer. People make also take an AI or ML bootcamp in order to upskill their tech career in software development or data science.
