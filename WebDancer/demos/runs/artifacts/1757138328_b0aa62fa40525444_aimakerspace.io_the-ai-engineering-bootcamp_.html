Frequently Asked Questions

Are the classes taught live?

Yes! Dr. Greg and The Wiz, along with a large team of 10+ peer supporters and course operations staff, run each and every class LIVE.

Are all live sessions recorded?

Yes! You can easily catch up asynchronously on the course if you need to miss a class or two, or if you're in a timezone that makes attending class live very difficult.

I work full-time, what is the expected time commitment?

This course is designed for people with full-time jobs, but it is a tough bootcamp. We recommend that if you do not have at least 10 hours per week on average to dedicate to the course, then you should wait until a cohort that you can commit to!

What additional costs should I budget for?

You will need to set up billing for the following tools:

  • OpenAI API access
  • Anthropic Claude API access
  • TogetherAI (for building with open-source models)

Recommended budget ~= $100 total

I am a not a hands-on coder, but I lead one or more teams building production LLM applications.

If you do not want to do engineering-level coding, you should not take this course. You may benefit from watching the conceptual and discussion portions of our YouTube videos as outlined in our free 2025 AI Engineering Roadmap.

However, if you lead engineers, the Bootcamp may be the perfect solution for your team. Check out the Teams Page for more information.

Do you offer any discounts for buying multiple seats?

YES! Please visit our Teams Page for more information.

Do you offer local pricing based on country?

We understand that it is difficult for folks around the world to afford our bootcamp, and while we look forward to serving local markets with local offerings led by local instructors in the future, this is not something that we are able to support as a small, bootstrapped startup today!

What is the policy for deferrals?

Please read more about our deferral policy here.

What is the policy for refunds?

We do not hesitate to give refunds. At the same time, we work hard to qualify each student before enrollment, and aim at a 0% withdrawal rate and refund rate in each cohort. Of course, life happens, and we promise to work with you when it does. Education is lifelong, and we'd like to join you on your AI career journey for the long haul..
